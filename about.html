About Us

Mission

Orbotics empowers students to innovate through robotics while uplifting our community with STEM outreach.


Who Are We?

Our team, Greeley Orbotics, is a student-run rookie FRC robotics team. Since our founding in 2023 we’ve grown to more than seventy diverse students. Beyond competition, we help members and the community build skills in design, software, construction, and business management.
